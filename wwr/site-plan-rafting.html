Overview

Purpose

Rafting Mountain, a renowned water rafting park in Lagos penninsula is bringing the rafting experience to you. Our presence online will make your favorite pastime sport much more easily accessible than ever before. You can book your adventure in advance, check our available rafts and get updates about competitions and event happening at the Rafting Mountain. we also provide tips and tricks for safe rafting as well as weather updates to help you plan your rafting successfully. We know how busy you life can get, you should not stress yourself in order to have fun.

Audience

The audience of the website include health conscious and agile indivdual within the age range of 16 to 50, who enjoy outdoor sporting experience, especially, rafting. They are involved in diverse works of life including white colar and blue colar jobs. They seek information regarding rafting, they are looking to book a rafting Trip and get tips on safe rafting. They primarily access information on desktop and mobile.
